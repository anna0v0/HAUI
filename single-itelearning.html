https://elc.home/eLearningProgram.aspx?org=970&prog=E1239 This courseware serves as a structured self-learning kit for staff who work with Diagnosis / Procedure data. It provides a fundamental knowledge on Terminology Management Principles, Hong Kong Clinical Terminology Table (HKCTT) with focus on Diagnosis and Procedure and its applications and maintenance. This courseware covers six chapters to cater for the learning need of staff with different job portfolios: 1. Hong Kong Clinical Terminology Table (HKCTT) - Diagnosis & Procedure 2. Reference terminologies for Diagnosis & Procedure and code assignment for International Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD) 3. Terminology management principles 4. Reporting Diagnoses & Provedures in Clinical Management System (CMS) 5. Principles in reporting Diagnosis & Procedure 6. Procedure categorization
